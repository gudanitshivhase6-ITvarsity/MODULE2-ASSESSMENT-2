About

Mall of the South is located in one of the wealthiest suburbs in the south of Jo’burg, particularly Pine city Hills, which attracts the affluent resident with a taste for luxury brands.


The 72 000 m² regional shopping centre is a retail market leader in mall innovation with an offering of over 160+ fabulous stores, free Wi-Fi and great visibility from two main arterial roads, namely Kliprivier Drive and Swartkoppies Road.

Pine cityoffers a high-end modern shopping experience where shoppers can enjoy an exciting tenant mix including all the major banks, anchor tenants Checkers, Woolworths, Pick ‘n Pay, Edgars, Game , Dischem, Clicks and more.
